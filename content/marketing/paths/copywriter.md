## who this is for
you write for a living, and ai already drafts half of it. you can prompt for a
paragraph. you can't yet build the pipeline that drafts a hundred on-voice ones,
or reliably catch the fact the model invented. this path gets you there.

## what you'll be able to do
build ai writing pipelines that run on their own. hold a brand voice by feeding
the model your style guide as context, not by hoping. and catch hallucinated
claims, names, and numbers before the copy ships, because a confident sentence
is not a true one.

## the path
twelve chapters: the python foundations, calling ai models, structured output,
prompting, context and retrieval, evals, and debugging broken ai output. about
10 to 12 hours, self-paced.

## how it works
no videos, no fizzbuzz. every step shows a small piece of ai-written code. you
read it, predict what it does, find the bug, fix it, run it. it all runs in
your browser.
