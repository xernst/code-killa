## who this is for
you run or work in support, and ai already drafts replies, routes tickets, and
answers customers. you need to understand what it's doing well enough to build
it, monitor it, and trust it. this path gets you there.

## what you'll be able to do
build a support agent that looks things up and answers with what it found.
structure tickets into clean data your system can route. read what the agent
actually did, and catch a wrong or off-policy answer before it reaches a
customer.

## the path
fifteen chapters: the python foundations, error handling, calling ai models,
structured output, agent loops, mcp, prompting, evals, and debugging broken ai
output. about 12 to 14 hours, self-paced.

## how it works
no videos, no fizzbuzz. every step shows a small piece of ai-written code. you
read it, predict what it does, find the bug, fix it, run it. it all runs in
your browser.
