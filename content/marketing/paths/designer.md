## who this is for
you design, and ai image and video tools are already part of the job. you can
generate one thing in a ui. you can't yet generate two hundred on-brand things
from code, or build the small tool that does it. this path gets you there.

## what you'll be able to do
generate images and video at scale from code, not by hand. build programmatic
design workflows that produce many on-brand variants from one layout. wire ai
generation into a real pipeline, and review the batch instead of trusting it.

## the path
eleven chapters: the python foundations, calling ai models, structured output,
prompting, and the image-generation, video-generation, and programmatic-design
chapters. about 9 to 11 hours, self-paced.

## how it works
no videos, no fizzbuzz. every step shows a small piece of ai-written code. you
read it, predict what it does, find the bug, fix it, run it. it all runs in
your browser.

## free, and for teams
free for individuals, forever. if your design org needs this at scale, with
progress dashboards and completion data, see [for teams](/for-teams).
