## who this is for
you work in hr or people ops. ai already touches hiring, onboarding, and policy,
and you're the one who has to decide how the whole org uses it safely. you need
to understand the tools well enough to govern them. this path gets you there.

## what you'll be able to do
understand what an ai team skill is and deploy one across an org. set access
policy and handle sensitive data and keys the right way. evaluate ai output for
people decisions, where a wrong answer is a real problem, not a bug. brief ai
tools clearly and know when a person has to stay in the loop.

## the path
eleven chapters: the python foundations, calling ai models, structured output,
prompting, secrets and access, team skills, and evals. about 9 to 11 hours,
self-paced.

## how it works
no videos, no fizzbuzz. every step shows a small piece of ai-written code. you
read it, predict what it does, find the bug, fix it, run it. it all runs in
your browser.
