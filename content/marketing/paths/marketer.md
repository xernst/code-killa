## who this is for
you run marketing, and ai already touches everything you do: copy, images,
campaign variants. you can prompt a chatbot, but you can't yet build the tool
that does it a thousand times. this path gets you there.

## what you'll be able to do
build ai content pipelines that run on their own. generate campaign creative,
images, and copy at scale from code. get structured data back from a model so
it drops straight into your tools. and qa what the model produced before it
ships, instead of trusting it.

## the path
ten chapters: the python foundations, calling ai models, structured output,
prompting, evals, and the image, video, and programmatic-design chapters. about
10 to 12 hours, self-paced. no software-engineering detours.

## how it works
no videos, no fizzbuzz. every step shows a small piece of ai-written code. you
read it, predict what it does, find the bug, fix it, run it. it all runs in
your browser.
