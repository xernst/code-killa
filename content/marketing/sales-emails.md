# Sales emails (copywriting brief, tier 2)

Cold outreach and deal-cycle emails for promptdojo corporate. Replace every
[bracketed] placeholder before sending. No em dashes, no banned words, no "hope
this finds you well." Each cold email is 80-150 words: hook, relevance, ask.

---

## Cold email A: HR director

**Subject:** reskilling [Company]'s team for ai

Hi [Name],

Every team at [Company] is using ai now, and most of your people learned it by
guessing. They can't tell when the output is wrong, and that gap is quiet right
up until it isn't.

That's a reskilling problem, and the usual fix (a one-hour webinar) doesn't
move it. promptdojo is a hands-on course that teaches people to build with ai
for real, with ten paths by role and dashboards that show you who actually
finished.

Worth 15 minutes to see if it fits what [Company] is planning for [next year /
this cycle]?
