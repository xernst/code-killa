# Career paths grid copy (tier 3)

The canonical career-paths grid: role, one-sentence value prop, time estimate,
link. Used on the homepage (section 4) and anywhere the ten paths are listed.
Per-path landing page copy lives in `content/marketing/paths/`.

Intro line: ten roles, ten routes through the course. pick the one closest to
your work. each one ends with something you can actually do.

| Role | Value prop | Time | Path page |
|---|---|---|---|
| developer | read, debug, and ship real software with ai instead of guessing. | 20 to 24 hours | /paths/developer |
| marketer | build campaign pipelines and generate creative at scale, yourself. | 10 to 12 hours | /paths/marketer |
| designer | program your own tools and batch-generate assets, no clicking through a ui. | 9 to 11 hours | /paths/designer |
| customer service | build support agents and catch their bad answers before customers do. | 12 to 14 hours | /paths/customer-service |
| copywriter | run ai writing pipelines that hold your voice and survive a fact-check. | 10 to 12 hours | /paths/copywriter |
| data analyst | pull, clean, and analyze data with ai doing the grunt work. | 13 to 15 hours | /paths/data-analyst |
| project manager | direct ai builds, review the output, and ship internal tools yourself. | 12 to 14 hours | /paths/project-manager |
| hr specialist | deploy team skills and set the rules for how your org uses ai. | 9 to 11 hours | /paths/hr-specialist |
| operations | automate the repetitive work your department runs on. | 16 to 18 hours | /paths/operations |
| lawyer | build document-review tools and catch the citations ai invents. | 13 to 15 hours | /paths/lawyer |

Each card's CTA reads **explore path** and links to the path page above.
